Frequently Ask Questions

Everything you need to know before your horseback riding tour.

Do I need horseback riding experience?

No experience is required. Our guided tours are beginner-friendly and suitable for all riders.

How long is the horseback riding tour?

The tour lasts approximately 1 hour, giving you plenty of time to enjoy the beach and take photos.

Are helmets provided?

Yes, safety helmets are provided for all riders during the tour.

Will we ride the horses into the water?

Yes! One of the highlights of the experience is riding the horses into the beautiful Caribbean Sea.

How do I book a tour?

You can easily book your tour by clicking the Book on WhatsApp button and messaging Andy directly.

Can beginners join the tour?

Yes, the tour is designed for both beginners and experienced riders.
